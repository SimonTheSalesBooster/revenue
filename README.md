# You're Leaving Money on the Table. This Finds It.

One founder ran `/revenue` and found $8K/month hiding in an underpriced service they'd been giving away as "part of the package."

Another discovered their best revenue opportunity wasn't a new product.. it was fixing a broken handoff between sales calls and proposals.

`/revenue` reads your entire business context -- notes, docs, emails, calendar, meeting transcripts -- and tells you exactly where revenue is leaking and what to build this week.

---

## Not generic advice. Your actual business.

Most AI revenue advice sounds like "start a newsletter" or "consider raising prices."

`/revenue` is different:

- Every suggestion cites data from your actual business
- Diagnoses WHY revenue isn't flowing before suggesting WHAT to sell
- No vague language -- instead of "significant revenue potential" you get "$5K-10K/month from packaging your sprint methodology as a licensed framework"
- Ends every run with artifacts Claude can build on the spot

---

## The 9-step analysis

1. **Deep context scan** -- Obsidian, Google Docs, Calendar, Gmail, Granola, Notion
2. **Asset inventory** -- skills, relationships, IP, audience, credibility signals
3. **Revenue diagnostics** -- conversion, revenue mix, sales system, pricing, product vs. service ratio
4. **Blind spot detection** -- market context, packaging gaps, positioning problems you can't see from inside
5. **Revenue opportunities** -- services, products, low-hanging fruit, undermonetized assets with dollar amounts
6. **Temporal tracking** -- compares against previous runs to avoid redundant suggestions
7. **Anti-pattern filtering** -- blocks generic advice, respects constraints, refuses to sugarcoat
8. **Prioritization** -- top 5 by effort-to-revenue ratio
9. **Actionable builds** -- pitch decks, pricing pages, outreach templates Claude builds immediately

---

## What you get back

**The Immediate Play** -- one thing to do this week with a specific first step

**The Biggest Upside** -- highest ceiling opportunity with evidence

**The Surprising One** -- the non-obvious play most people and most AIs would miss

**The Structural Fix** -- one change that impacts all future revenue

**Actionable Builds** -- documents Claude creates on the spot

---

## Usage

```
/revenue              # Full revenue analysis
/revenue [domain]     # Focused on a specific area
```

---

## Install

```bash
mkdir -p .claude/commands
cp revenue.md .claude/commands/revenue.md
```

Then run `/revenue` inside Claude Code.

Also works in Claude Cowork or any Claude-powered environment.
